Evening Tales for the Winter: Case Complete!. Evening Tales for the Winter is an 1856 anthology, compiled by Henry St. Clair of "Wonderful & Supernatural Stories," many of which were translated from German sources. Neither the translators nor original authors/stories are credited. Dark Tales Sleuth on the case!
